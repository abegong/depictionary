Rules

Object: Score points by depicting and guessing as many words as possible.

Turns: Players take turns as the depictor. As depictor, you get 1 minute and 10 words. Your goal is to get the other players to guess as many words as possible within one minute. You have 1 minute to get 0 words and 1 minute. This is a fast-paced, creative game. Everyone is always playing.

Object: No words or letters. You can pantomime, draw pictures, make sounds effects—anything except use words, letters, and numbers. (When in doubt, anything that appears on a standard keyboard is off-limits.)

Scoring: When you are the depictor, you score 3 points for every word guessed. When you are guessing, you score one point for each word you guess.

Teams: there are no teams
